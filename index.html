Biography

Pastor Edwin Biayeibo is a dynamic minister of the Gospel, called to raise leaders and transform lives through the preaching of God's Word. As the resident pastor of House on the Rock Enugu, he oversees a thriving congregation with a vision to impact society through sound biblical teaching and practical Christian living.

With over 15 years of ministry experience, Pastor Edwin is passionate about helping individuals discover their God-given purpose and walk in divine wisdom. His messages are known for their depth, relevance, and transformative power.
